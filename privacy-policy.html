Privacy Policy

At Hernández y Mateo Construcciones we respect the privacy of everyone who contacts us through our website. This policy explains how we handle the information received via the contact form.

1. Purpose of the contact form

The sole purpose of the form is to facilitate communication between website visitors and our company. The data sent is used exclusively to respond to the received request or inquiry.

2. Data processed

The only data requested are those necessary to maintain basic contact (for example, name and means of communication). No postal addresses or sensitive personal information are requested.

3. Processing and storage

Messages sent through the form are managed via the secure Netlify Forms service. Once the communication is received, the data is used solely to respond to the client and is deleted after processing. It is not stored, analyzed, or shared with third parties.

4. Data disclosure

No data is transferred or disclosed to third parties. The information remains within the communication channel established between the sender and Hernández y Mateo Construcciones.

5. User rights

The user may request the immediate deletion of messages sent through the form at any time, simply by notifying us via the site's contact email address.

6. Security

The form uses encrypted connections (HTTPS) and is processed through Netlify's security mechanisms, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of the information during transmission.

7. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this policy to adapt it to possible regulatory or technical changes. The current version is always available on this page.
